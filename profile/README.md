### 👋 Namaste, I’m Upesh — building from a wooden house in Lamahatta, Darjeeling

I grew up imagining phones that could play movies (2010) and remote work before it was normal.  
Now I’m building the optimistic 2050 future I described in **[Imagination of the Future](https://github.com/ripplewave2025/ripplewave2025/blob/main/Imaginationofthefuture.md)** — one village at a time.

**Core mission**  
Create **Halo** — a hyper-personalized AI (local-first, privacy-first, offline-capable) that knows *only* you: every voice note, 4 a.m. thought, childhood memory, goal you ever wrote. It executes like you would, teaches you subconsciously, preserves your stories for your grandchildren, and turns “I don’t know tech” into “I just told my computer what I want.”

Everything I ship is designed for the underserved Himalayas first — then everywhere.

**What humans still do in 2030–2050**  
- Direction, emotion, curiosity, wonder  
- Ask the right questions  
- Feel the pride when AI validates an idea born from a village spring or waste tea leaves  

AI does the heavy lifting. We keep the soul.

## 🌲 Now Building in Lamahatta (honest status — April 2026)

- **AIforStudents.online**  
  Piloting in Lamahatta government school.  ₹2000 one-time setup aligns with NEP 2020. Open-source manuals + simple AI tools in Nepali/Hindi/English so any teacher or student can run it. Kids are already inventing filters from pine needles and forever-batteries from tea leaves. I’m just the guy who wired the first laptop.

- **Gorkhayai.com & HomestaySamaj**  
  Free Vercel websites + subdomains for homestays and restaurants that have never been online. Reasonable paid upgrades (domain, SOPs, grant applications, social media, booking flows, PMS, influencer reach, local talent booking). Revenue funds the next piece: **Encyclopedia Galactica** — a conversational agent that lets anyone in Darjeeling talk to the living memory of our fathers, grandfathers, and grandmothers. History, wisdom, bitterness, jokes, and yes… even “what if Gorkhaland?”

- **Desktop Halo (Tech Support that talks)**  
  Voice-first app for grandmas, uncles, and anyone who still says “computer bhariyo.” You speak once. It personalizes your entire Windows machine, sets up Halo, books doctor appointments, pays bills, and never makes you feel tech-illiterate again. Safety, privacy, and offline mode baked in.

- **Hyper-Personalized Assistant (the real Halo)**  
  Text → Voice. Trained only on *your* life data. Updates your goals daily. Slips in the exact paragraph from *Rich Dad Poor Dad* (or any book) exactly when your brain is ready. Tracks what you remember. After you’re gone, it still advises your children exactly like you would. This is the moat. This is the legacy.

- **Village Engine** — [lamahatta.vercel.app](https://lamahatta.vercel.app)  
  Converging talent, grants, sponsors, and mutual care so one village becomes self-sustaining — then connects to the next. Economy, learning, culture, and opportunity that runs itself. Scaling blueprint for all of Darjeeling and beyond.

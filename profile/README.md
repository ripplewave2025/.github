### I’m Upesh — building the optimistic 2050 future from Lamahatta, Darjeeling

I once imagined phones that could play movies inside them (2010).  
I lived remote work before the world did.  

Now I’m writing the blueprint for a world where **every Windows user — especially in India and the Himalayas — has their own Halo**.

**Halo** is my hyper-personalized AI:  
- Trained only on *your* life (notes, voice memos, 4 a.m. thoughts, dreams)  
- Runs locally when possible, works offline, respects privacy  
- Turns grandmas, students, freelancers, and village teachers into confident tech users  
- Becomes your legacy — still advising your grandchildren exactly like you would  

**Mission**  
Expand human agency from Darjeeling outward. AI does the heavy lifting (data, execution, validation). Humans keep the wonder, curiosity, emotion, and the right questions.

## 🌲 What I’m Building Right Now (April 2026 — honest status)

- **AIforStudents.online**  
  Piloting in Lamahatta government school. ₹2000 one-time setup + open-source manuals aligned with NEP 2020. Any teacher can run it. Kids are already inventing pine-needle water filters and tea-leaf forever-batteries.

- **Gorkhayai.com & HomestaySamaj**  
  Free Vercel websites + subdomains for homestays and restaurants that have never been online. Paid upgrades (domains, SOPs, grants, bookings, local talent) fund the next layer: **Encyclopedia Galactica** — a conversational agent preserving Darjeeling’s fathers, grandfathers, wisdom, stories, bitterness, and jokes.

- **Desktop Halo (Voice Tech Support)**  
  Talk to your computer once. It personalizes everything, sets up Halo, books appointments, pays bills — no more “tech illiterate” feeling. Privacy-first, offline-ready.

- **Hyper-Personalized Halo (the core)**  
  Text → Voice. Knows you better than anyone. Slips in the exact lesson or book quote when your brain is ready. Tracks memory. Lives on after you as your digital legacy.

- **Village Engine** → [lamahatta.vercel.app](https://lamahatta.vercel.app)  
  Talent convergence, grants, mutual care, self-sustaining economy. One village → connected villages → self-sustaining Darjeeling blueprint.

# Namaste, I'm Upesh.

I am trying to use AI, software, documentation, and practical systems to make underserved communities more capable — starting from Darjeeling.

Most of what I build is early. Some parts are live, some are pilots, some are still ideas being tested in public. I would rather be honest about that than pretend everything is finished.

## What I am working on

### [AIforStudents.online](https://aiforstudents.online)
Helping underserved schools get online in a simple, low-cost way and align with **NEP 2020** where possible.

- Pilot work is centered around **Lamahatta**
- I am trying to make digital systems schools can actually run
- I want the manuals and operations to be open enough that people can manage them without depending on me forever
- If support is needed, I can help — but the goal is self-reliance

### [GorkhayAI](https://gorkhayai.com)
An umbrella for practical local tools and long-term cultural work.

#### [Homestay Samaj](https://homestaysamaj.gorkhayai.com/)
Free websites for homestays and restaurants that do not yet have a proper online presence.

- Using free infrastructure, subdomains, and community resources where possible
- Helping with domains, SOPs, grant documents, hiring structure, operations, social setup, booking flow, billing, PMS software, and local promotion
- Organising local talent across travel, catering, banquets, live music, and support services
- Where I charge, the goal is not extraction. The goal is to keep the work sustainable and push more resources back into mission work

#### Cultural memory and conversational agents
A long-term effort to preserve and digitise the knowledge, memory, language, wisdom, stories, and realities of Darjeeling and Gorkha people.

I want this to become something future generations can talk to — not as entertainment, but as a serious archive of people, place, memory, truth, and lived experience.

Accuracy matters more to me than virality.

### Desktop Tech Support
A project around making computers easier for people who do not feel comfortable with technology.

The idea is a personalised support layer that can understand the person, ask the right questions, set things up safely, and help them use the computer without panic or confusion.

### Hyper-personalised assistant
This is one of the most important things I am building.

The goal is an assistant that knows your goals, tracks what matters to you, and teaches you the right thing at the right time. If a book, technique, or idea is useful for your actual life, it should bring that back when relevant and help you remember it.

- Working on **text first**, then **voice**
- Building the architecture and memory templates
- I think this can become useful for students, teachers, and people trying to learn deeply
- It still needs constant updating, debugging, and safety work

### [Village Engine / Lamahatta](https://lamahatta.vercel.app/)
An experiment in building a more self-sustaining village model.

The goal is to converge talent, look for grants, connect sponsors where needed, support local businesses, and create systems where people can help each other while the village becomes more economically and digitally capable.

If it works in one village, it should be repeatable in others.

## Principles

- Build for people who are usually ignored
- Use simple tools before expensive tools
- Make systems people can operate themselves
- Prefer accuracy over noise
- Keep humans in the loop
- Preserve what is worth carrying forward
- Stay honest about what is finished, what is broken, and what is still a hypothesis
